Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Savory Sphaghetti bolognese

A restaurant class masterchef, dish. Make at home and feel the sweet taste explode in your mouth and bring a smile on your families' faces.

Ingredients

  • 500g lean ground minced beef
  • 400g dried spaghetti
  • 1 large diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large carrot grated/diced
  • 800g chopped tomatoes
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp dried olegano
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Saute aromatics

    2. Heat oil in saucepan, add diced onion and garlic, cook for about 3 minutes to soft and fragrant. Stir in grated carrot and cook for 2 minutes.

  2. Brown beef

    3. Add minced beef in pan,cook for 5-6 minutes until meat browned.

  3. Build the sauce

    4. Stir in tomato paste, dried oregano and crumble beff stockk cube. Pour in chopped tomatoes mix and bring mixture to a boil.

  4. Simmer and thicken

    5. Turn heat to low, cover and let sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes.

  5. Cook the spaghetti

    6. Bring pot of salted water to a boil the add spaghetti and cool till its al dente then drain.

  6. Combine and serve

    7. Divide spaghetti in plates then generously spoon bolognese sauce over them. Top each plate with cheese and crack fresh black pepper.
