French fries Recipe

Tasty French Fries

Make perfect restaurant quality fries at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 large peeled and cut uniform potato sticks
  • Soak potatoes in cold water 30-60 minutes then rinse and dry
  • 1-2 litres vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Place potatoes in pot.
  2. Add vinegar and bsp salt then cover with cold water.
  3. Boil over high heat then reduce to gentle simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Carefully remove fries with spoon, spread them on paper towel and steam dry.
  5. Pour oil into pot and heat to 150 degrees
  6. Add fries in small batches, fry for 4-5 minutes until cooked but not brown.
  7. Remove fries and dry them on paper towel lined tray, let them cool.
  8. Reheat oil to 200 degrees.
  9. Fry cooled fries in batches 2-4 minutes until deep brown and crispy.
  10. Remove and drain on paper towel and toss with salt while hot and serve
