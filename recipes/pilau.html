Pilau Recipe

Swahili Beef-Pilau

This pilau recipe yields a deeply aromatic, spiced one-pot rice dish with tender beef.

Ingredients

For the meat:

  • 500g beef(cubed)
  • 1 medium sized onions(sliced)
  • 4 cloves garlic & 1 inch peice ginger (paste)
  • 1 tbsp salt

For the pilau masala mix:

  • 2 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp cardamon
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cloves

For the rice:

  • 2cups long grain Basmati rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 medium red onions sliced
  • 2 beef stock cubes
  • 4cups water

Instructions

  1. Prepare and brown the beef:

    2. Add cubed beef, a little water, sliced onion, half garlic-ginger paste and pinch of salt in pot then boil till meat is tender and all water evaporates .Add vegetable oil to pot,fry until beef dark

  2. Saute aromatics & spices:

    3. Add remaining paste and spices, stir about 1-2 minutes then add remaining sliced onion and saute till soft. Add the pilau masala and stir for another 30 seconds.

  3. Toast the rice:

    4. Wash the rice till water runs clear. Pour into pot and stir, toast for about 2minutes ensuring all grains coated in oil.

  4. Cook:

    5. Pour in the water and add beef stock cubes. Bring to a boil. Cover pot with tight lid, lower heat to absolute mininmum and steam.

  5. Rest and serve:

    6. Turn off heat then rest pilau covered for 5-10 minutes then serve hot.
